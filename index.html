Services

Web Application Testing

We measure the security posture of your website and/or your customized web app. We focus on identifying the weak points in the entire web app in order to guarantee the security of it and your data. We strictly follow the testing guides from OWASP.

Cyber Intelligence

We find and detect threats and risks that standard antivirus and simple firewalls cannot. Finding new attack vectors and bringing them to your attention before they become a problem is one of our key values. We work in this area with our partner HakDefNet GmbH, leader in the world of Cyber ​​Intelligence.

Education & Training

Humans are often the weakest link in the security of an organization. We offer seminars and/or workshops to educate you and your employees on how to protect themselves and your organization from common and novel attacks.

Vulnerability Scanning

An expert performs a scan to search your systems for known vulnerabilities with the ultimate goal of providing you with a remediation report prioritized on risk.

Web/Mobile Application Development

We create customized, accessible and secure applications that focus on solving the specific needs and objectives of your business.

Consulting

We are pleased to provide to you information and advise enriched with the latest research of the Industry for the success of your next project. For a free initial consultation Contact us.
